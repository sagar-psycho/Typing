Frequently Asked Questions

A typing speed test measures how fast and accurately you can type a given piece of text.

Typing speed is typically measured in words per minute (WPM), which is the number of words typed correctly in one minute.

Accuracy is important because typing quickly is not useful if many errors are made. High accuracy ensures that the typed text is correct and reliable.
